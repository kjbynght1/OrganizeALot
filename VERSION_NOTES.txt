OrganizeALot Residential Inspection Assistant v1.8

- Changed the Sketch tab into a Measurement Sketch Builder.
- Enter wall length, then tap a direction button.
- Direction buttons include:
  - Up, Down, Left, Right
  - Diagonal angled directions: NE, NW, SE, SW
- Added Custom Angled Wall option.
- Added Close, Undo, Reset, Center/Fit, Save Sketch.
- Labels each wall with its measurement.
- Calculates approximate perimeter.
- Export ZIP now includes:
  - Sketch/measurement-sketch.png
  - Sketch/measurements.txt

- Roof/shingle detail protection
- Exact residential required photo list
- Required photo progress counter
- Auto-correct sideways/rotated photos
- Green photo buttons
- GPS/date reading
- Organized ZIP export
